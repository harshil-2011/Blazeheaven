Server Commands

Jobs Plugin

  • /job - Open the job menu
  • /job browse - Browse available jobs
  • /job join [job] - Join a specific job
  • /job leave - Leave your current job

Shop Commands

  • /shop or /sh - Open the shop menu
  • /sell hand - Sell items in your hand
  • /sell all - Sell all eligible items in your inventory

Economy Commands

  • /balance or /bal - Check your balance
  • /pay [player] [amount] - Send money to another player

Skin Commands

  • /skin [username] - Apply a player's skin
  • /skin url [link] - Apply skin from URL
  • /skin reset - Reset to your default skin
