Commands for Dark Warriors SMP

Jobs Plugin

  • /job - Open the job menu.
  • /job browse - Open the help menu for job-related commands.
  • /job leave - Leave your current job.
  • /job category [category] - Leave a specific job category.

Shop Commands

  • /shop or /sh - Open the shop menu.
  • /sell all hand - Sell all items currently in your hand.

    • Balance Commands

    • /balance - Check your current balance.

    Skin Commands

    • /skin [username] - Apply the skin of the specified username.
    • /skin url (paste the link of skin) - This apply skin from the url of skin.
