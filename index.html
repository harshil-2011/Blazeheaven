Server Rules

To ensure everyone has a great experience, please follow these guidelines:

  • No griefing - Respect other players' builds and properties.
  • No stealing - Don't take items without permission.
  • Be respectful - No harassment or discrimination.
  • No cheating - This includes hacked clients or x-ray.
  • No PvP without consent - Always ask before combat.
  • Keep the world tidy - Clean up after yourself.
  • No spamming - In chat or with entities.
  • No inappropriate builds - Keep it family-friendly.
  • Respect staff decisions - Discuss issues politely.
  • Have fun! - Help create a positive community!
