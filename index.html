Server Rules

Follow these rules to ensure everyone has a great time

Strictly Prohibited

  • No hacking, cheating, or exploiting glitches
  • No griefing or stealing from others
  • No racism, sexism, or hate speech
  • No spamming or excessive caps
  • No advertising other servers

Expected Behavior

  • Be respectful to all players
  • Use claims to protect your builds
  • Keep the chat family-friendly
  • Report bugs to staff immediately
  • Have fun and be creative!

Consequences

Breaking these rules will result in punishments ranging from temporary bans to permanent removal from the server depending on severity. Our staff team reserves the right to punish any behavior deemed inappropriate even if not explicitly listed here.
